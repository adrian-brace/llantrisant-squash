Menu

    Use these fields to narrow down the player selection. The club/county/country fields are filled in dynamically as you select them so it's quick and easy to narrow down to a single club - select your country first and work back to your club.

    Enter the player's name or parts of a player's name into the box labelled 'Search name'. As you enter a player's name, a list of suggestions will appear below. You can click on one of those names to go straight to that player's history.

    Click on Show to apply the filter.

     Use damped levels (for seedings)

    Many of the fields shown in the rankings are links. Click on the player's name to go to their history page. Click on country, club or event to see those player rankings. Click on the last match date to see more details for that match.

    Level confidence is the confidence the system has in the player's level. It goes up with more matches, down with unexpected results.
